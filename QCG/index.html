Who are we ?

the Quantum Computing Group (QCG)at IIT Roorkee is a student-driven initiative focused on exploring and advancing quantum computing in the world. through research, events, and collaborations.

The Quantum Computing Group (QCG)at IIT Roorkee works by organizing research, workshops, and events focused on quantum computing. They explore quantum algorithms, quantum machine learning, and cryptography while collaborating with experts and industry leaders. They also conduct seminars, hackathons, and projects to help students understand and apply quantum computing concepts. üöÄ

The primary aim of the Quantum Computing Group (QCG) at IIT Roorkee is to advance research and awareness in quantum computing by fostering a community of students interested in quantum technologies, conducting workshops, seminars, and projects, and collaborating with industry leaders to explore real-world applications.
